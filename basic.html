By India Today Business Desk: Over 1,500 Air India pilots have written to Tata Sons chairman emeritus Ratan Tata, seeking his intervention after the airline revamped their salary structure without any prior consultantion. The pilots, who have voiced their dissatisfaction against the airline management’s decision to unilaterally alter their salary structure and service conditions, said the concerns of pilots are not being addressed by the airline’s HR team.
